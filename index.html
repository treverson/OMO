Concept of OMO token
1. How do you make money?

Commissions from buy, sell and transfer movements of OMO tokens.

Commissions from your referral links.

Dividends from subsequent games built on the OMO tokens.

2. What is it «OMO Token»?

OMO is a decentralized infrastructure project created by a professional community in cryptography science, finance and investment-banking.

OMO creates an ecosystem of active crypto-investors based on the TRON blockchain, which can guarantee transparency, safety and liquidity.

OMO owners can get a return in OMO tokens, convertible to TRX when there are new platform users, new services implementation and partnership program’s cooperation.

3. Mathematical model

Commission fee for deposit: 10% of the deposit amount.

Fee for withdrawal: 4% from the amount.

Commission fee for transfer to another wallet: 1% from transferred amount.

Example - You have bought 10 000 OMO for 100 TRX, than 10 TRX will be proportionally distributed through all OMO owners.

33% of 10% charging fees sharing between users who has a referral link.

According to this model token pricing balancing by supply and demand from ecosystem’s users, herewith token is secured from manipulation.
