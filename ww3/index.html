How it works

How to Play Whale War 3 (WW3)


To participate in a round, you must purchase WW3 keys. Every key adds 30 seconds to the Grand Pot Timer (to a cap of 24:00). The Grand Pot Timer starts counting down from 24:00. When it reaches 00:00, the round ends and the Grand Pot is given to the last key buyer.


Daily Whale Pot: The Daily Whale Pot is distributed every 24 hours to the top 10 key buyers in the last 24 hours (ranked by the number of keys bought). The Daily Whale Pot Timer is unaffected by key purchases.


Key holders’ dividend: Key holders receive dividends from subsequent key purchases. The amount of dividend is proportional to the number of keys. All keys are reset when a round ends.



Key purchase distribution

5% to referral commission
30% to WW3 key holders in the round
15% to the Daily Dividends. Every day, 20% of the Daily Dividends rewards the top 10 key buyers in the last 24 hours.
10% to the next pot
25% to the grand pot

Note to closed beta participants: instead of 24 hours, all the timers are 3 hours.
